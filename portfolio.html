Portfolio

The project:

To create a network, I wanted to demonstrate my project skills. In order to do this, I have designed a a portfolio website, which you are currently viewing.

To begin with, I created a wireframe, followed by a design from the wireframe, and finally I started the development. My name is at the top of the website. I wanted to use a unique font, and changed the background from black to a lighter colour, as I feel this is more aesthetically pleasing, and which I feel enhances the appearance.

Although I enjoyed making a design for my own website, I found the development more interesting and challenging.
